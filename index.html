organisation

Quests

Through some exercises, "Quests time" is the occasion to discover new concepts on your own, before studying them all together with our trainer.
It is also the time for us to share our knowledge and know-hows, all the exercises being corrected by the students themselves - no trainer's correction.

Lessons

A theorical lesson is given by the trainer once a week to explain us the new concepts that were subject of the quests we were working on.

Workshops

Following a lesson, the workshop is a pratical exercise whose aim is to explore a new topic, step by step.

Projects

During the trainee, 3 main projects are organised by the trainers team. The purpose is to work in small groups of 5 students to build a project as "in real life". In collaboration with some professional partners, the last project will finally have to be delivered to the customer.

methodology

Scrum Method

Product Backlog The Product Owner meet the client to analyse his needs and identify the features of the product (user stories)

The Sprint The SCRUM method is characterized by a distribution of each of the tasks to be done. The team sorts the features and tasks which it divides into Sprints (cycle duration of two weeks).

Review Every Friday, we test the feature with the Product Owner. We make a demo of what has been created. Then the recipient of the feature confirms or not whether the functionality works as desired. The circle is complete !

Wild Method

Project Plan At the start of the projet we identify the differents features and then split them into sections that we attribute to the members of the team.

Daily Stand-Up Everyday we talk about the work and difficulties we had the past day and what we plan to do the present day.

Retro FridayEvery friday we do a retrospective of the week and show the advancement of the project, then talk about the next steps ahead.
