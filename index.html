organisation

Quests

Through some exercises, "Quests time" is the occasion to discover new concepts on your own, before studying them all together with the trainer.
It is also the time for us to share our knowledges and know-hows, all the exercises being corrected by the students tehmselves - no trainer's correction.

Lessons

A theorical lesson is given by the trainer once a week to explain us the new concepts that were subject of the quests we were working on.

Workshops

Following a lesson, the workshop is a pratical exercise whose aim is to explore a new topic, step by step.

Projects

During the trainee, we are going to work on 3 main projects organised by the trainers board. The purpose is to work in small groups of 4/5 students to build a project as "in real life".
In collaboration with some companies, the last project is based on real customers needs and will finally have to be delivered

methodology

In this section we will compare the Scrum method to the Wild code School Method !

Scrum Method

Product Backlog

The Product Owner meet the client to analyse his needs and identify the features of the product (user stories)

The Sprint

The SCRUM method is characterized by a distribution of each of the tasks to be done. The team sorts the features and tasks which it divides into Sprints (cycle duration of two weeks).

Sprint Review

Every Friday we test the feature with the Product Owner. We make a demo of what has been created. Then the recipient of the feature confirms or not whether the functionality works as desired. The circle is complete !

Wild Method

Project Plan

At the start of the projet we identify the differents features and then split them into sections that we attribute to the members of the team

Daily Stand-Up

Everyday we talk about the work and difficulties we had the past day and what we plan to do the present day.

Retro Friday

Every friday we do a retrospective of the week and show the advancement of the project, then talk about the next steps ahead
